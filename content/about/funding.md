The IBL Core is supported by a new 5-year grant from the Simons Foundation, starting January 1, 2026, enabling the expansion of its scientific and technical activities.

In parallel, a 3-year Wellcome Trust grant, beginning April 1, 2026, supports outreach and community engagement efforts. In addition, a number of ongoing projects, supported by the NIH, are expected to conclude in 2026-2027.
