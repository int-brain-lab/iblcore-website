The IBL Core supports a diverse portfolio of projects spanning its original scientific mission and new collaborative efforts. Ongoing projects include those emerging from the IBL, building on established large-scale datasets and infrastructure.

New partner projects reflect collaborations developed through the IBL Core model, extending our tools and expertise to external groups. In parallel, our Affiliate Programme connects a broader community of researchers through lighter-touch engagement and shared best practices.
