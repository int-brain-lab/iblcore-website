The IBL Core Engineering Team is a central group of scientific engineers dedicated to enabling large-scale, collaborative neuroscience. Building on the International Brain Laboratory's model of open, distributed science, the Core brings together expertise in data, software, and infrastructure to support research across laboratories worldwide. What We Do

The core team develops and maintains the technical foundations that make collaborative neuroscience possible:

Data Infrastructure & Access

Building tools for standardized data ingestion, organization and access.

Processing Pipelines

Developing robust, scalable workflows for electrophysiology, imaging, behavior and video.

Cloud & Web Platforms

Creating shared infrastructure and web-based tools.

Visualization & Software

Designing user interfaces, applications and libraries.

Hardware & Acquisition Support

Providing guidance and solutions for experimental setups and data acquisition systems.

Training & Community

Supporting the broader community through workshops, documentation and open standards for best practices.
